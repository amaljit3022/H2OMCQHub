About H2OMCQHub

H2OMCQHub is a free and open-source learning platform dedicated to professionals, engineers, and students in the water supply and sanitation sector. Built under the vision of The Krittika Project, it aims to simplify access to over 25,000 MCQs curated from official manuals, advisories, and operational guidelines issued by CPHEEO, MOHUA, Ministry of Jal Shakti, and SBM(G).

The platform encourages self-paced, focused learning across manuals, categorized by topic and year, with features such as:

  • Manual-wise MCQ practice with explanations
  • Bookmarking for revision
  • Progress tracking and scores
  • Instant feedback and certificate generation (coming soon)

Whether you're a field engineer preparing for a departmental review or a student aiming to master public infrastructure standards — this platform is for you.

This initiative is part of The Krittika Project — an open knowledge movement dedicated to building free, AI-supported, and GIS-enabled public utilities with purpose and empathy.
